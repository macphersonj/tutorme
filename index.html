Services Offered

Private Tutoring

Private tutoring sessions allow the student to work in one-on-one sessions with the Tutor. Students study at their own pace and will be offered an individualised education program to best suit their needs.

Group Tuition

Group tuition is also available. This miniature classroom environment delivers well prepared lessons for small groups of 3-4 students. Students must be in the same subject area and of the same age group.

Language Support

Students may also wish to receive English language support to enhance their school work. Lessons can range from improving general English conversation, writing and grammar to more advanced lessons on improving essay writing and formatting.

Special Educational Needs

Services are also offered to students with Special Educational Needs. The Tutor is experienced in working with children with a variety of Special Educational Needs such as ADD, ADHD, Asperger's, dyslexia and dyscalculia.
